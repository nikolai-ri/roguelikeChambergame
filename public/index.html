Instructions:

  • Fight the red boxes to increase XP, level up and attack power!
  • Find the yellow boxes to get better weapons. Find the green boxes to gain health.
  • Find the purple box, to enter the next dungeon.
  • Fight the final enemy in the third dungeon. But beware, it will come to you and once he finds you, you cannot escape the fight!
  • Score is based on your XP and the time you take to kill the final enemy.
